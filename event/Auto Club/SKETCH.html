Introduction

Are you a person who sketches down everything you see? Or are you the one who masters sketching? This event is specially conducted for you. Here you're going to sketch on the topics we give. The event contains two rounds.
In the first round you're asked to sketch down the car body parts, and the details will be shared through a google classroom on the day of the event. You're asked to sketch the vehicle components in the next round. And finally, you're asked to submit the sketch by scanning and uploading it in the google classroom.

Rules and Regulations

ROUND

● Selection of the winner will be done after assessing all the sketches

● Dimensions and neatness are very important as it is a professional sketching event.

Time: 2 hours

ROUND

● The sketches will be automobile related. Details of which will be given on the day of the event.

● Participants whose sketch satisfies all the details provided by us will be qualified as the winner.

● Judges decision will be final.
