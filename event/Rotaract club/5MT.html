Introduction

Are you the kind of a person who opts for short, exciting and enlightening events. Then here is your genre. The Rotaract club of Sathyabama organises an event called 5MT. Present a paper, within 5 minutes, on any topic you wish. Presenting paper is not so easy. And not everyone can do it, you need to research about the topic and have the skill to present it effectively with the right body language and mind-set.

Rules and Regulations

There will be 3 rounds totally


  • Round 1: The document is analysed and further checked for plagiarism.

  • Round 2: you will be presenting your paper.

  • Round 3: The topic or idea of your paper will be judged based on the implementation of the idea.

  • The winning criteria for this event is based on the percentage acquired from all the 3 rounds together.

  • Top 3 people with higher percentages will be considered winners.

  • Judges decision will be final.
